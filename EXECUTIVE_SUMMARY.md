# Executive Summary

## Business Problem

Retail organizations often lack consolidated visibility into sales performance across regions, segments, and product categoriesâ€”making it difficult to identify underperforming areas, track profitability trends, and understand return patterns that erode margins.

**Challenges:**
- Regional performance discrepancies hidden in raw data
- Profit margins vulnerable to untracked discount erosion
- Return patterns not linked to manager accountability
- Lack of trend visibility prevents proactive decision-making

## Impact

A well-designed dashboard transforms reactive decision-making into proactive strategy:

- **Revenue Opportunity**: Quickly identify high-performing categories and regions to expand
- **Risk Mitigation**: Surface underperforming segments before losses compound
- **Inventory Optimization**: Use segment and category insights for smarter allocation
- **Accountability**: Link manager performance to regional returns and profitability
- **Trend Analysis**: Year-over-year comparisons enable early intervention on negative trends

## Solution

This Tableau dashboard solution provides:

### **Year-Based Dynamic Filtering**
- Interactive parameter (2014-2017) allows comparison across time periods
- Enables trend analysis and YOY insights without static reports
- Stakeholders can drill into specific years for detailed investigation

### **Multi-Level Analysis**
- Sales Analysis Dashboard: Regional, segment, and category performance
- Profit Analysis Dashboard: Margin trends and profitability deep-dives
- Year Over Year Dashboard: Period-over-period comparisons with growth rates

### **Linked Data Intelligence**
- **Returns Data Integration**: Surfaces return rates by order, revealing quality issues and manager accountability
- **People Data Linkage**: Maps managers to regions for performance accountability
- **Customer Segmentation**: Distinct behavior patterns across Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office segments

### **Actionable Insights**
- Identify top 3 profit-generating categories to prioritize
- Spot underperforming regions requiring intervention
- Track discount impact on profitability
- Monitor return rates as quality and customer satisfaction indicator

## Outcomes

By implementing this dashboard, leadership can:
1. Make data-driven decisions in real-time instead of waiting for monthly reports
2. Quickly respond to performance changes with year-based filtering
3. Allocate resources strategically based on regional and category insights
4. Hold managers accountable through return and regional performance visibility
5. Reduce margin erosion by monitoring discount patterns

## Data Foundation

- **9,995+ sales transactions** analyzed across 2014-2017
- **Three main data sources**: Orders (transaction detail), People (manager mapping), Returns (quality tracking)
- **Complete dimensions**: Region, Segment, Category, Sub-Category, Product, Customer
